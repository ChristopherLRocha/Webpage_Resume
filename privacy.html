Privacy Policy

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle any information you provide while using our website. We do not collect or store any personal information unless you choose to contact us.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from visitors to this website. If you choose to contact us via a contact form or email, the only data we collect is the information you provide in your message (such as your name, email address, and message content).

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide will only be used to respond to your inquiries or requests. We will not share your information with third parties or use it for any other purpose.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes. We do not store any personal information in cookies.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services (such as Google Analytics, social media plugins, or embedded content) on this site.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure the security of your data. However, please be aware that no method of data transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy or how we handle your information, please contact me.
